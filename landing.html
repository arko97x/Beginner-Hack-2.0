I've never really maintained a blog before! Should be fun, though! :)

Just to take you through how each post is structured, the stuff in the rounded-gray box is the tag that the post belongs to. It should help you navigate through this online residence. :)

I personally, am a big fan of minimalism. This blog is designed along similar lines. There is one other thing that I'm rather particular about - I hate websites that open up fresh content in the same tab. Coz, if you need to move back, you obviously need to move all the way up, to the upper left/right corner of your screen and click on the designated button. (I find this whole concept rather *shitty*) I like to stay on one single page and have all the content presented to me without navigating to a new tab or reloading the page.

The current theme/design of the blog has tried to incorporate this very concept! And with that, we come to the end of the very first blogpost. I shall say no more. Watch this space for more content in the near future.
